Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information CalcBet collects, how it’s used, and your choices. We aim to keep things simple and transparent.

No betting advice Educational tools No account required

1) Information we collect

Calculator inputs: Values you type into calculators (stake, odds, etc.) are used to compute results in your browser.

Contact form: If you send a message, we receive what you submit (name, email, message) to respond.

Basic analytics (optional): We may collect non-identifying usage data (like page visits) to improve the site.

2) What we don’t do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not require an account to use calculators.
  • We do not store your calculator entries as personal betting history.

3) Cookies

If analytics or embedded services are enabled, they may use cookies or similar technologies. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

4) Data security

We use reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5) Your choices

  • You can avoid sending the contact form if you don’t want to share email/name.
  • You can disable cookies through browser settings.
  • You can request deletion of contact messages you’ve sent.

6) Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: calcbetcare01@gmail.com

7) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date on this page.
